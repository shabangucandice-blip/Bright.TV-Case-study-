A data-driven strategy to accelerate subscription base growth for the current fiscal year. This analysis identifies high-potential audience segments, optimises conversion funnels, and develops predictive models to reduce subscriber churn. 

🎯 Primary Objective

To achieve a measurable increase in the total active subscriber base by optimising the Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and maximising the Trial-to-Paid Conversion Rate. 
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
New Subscription Growth Rate: Monthly percentage increase in new sign-ups.
Trial Conversion Rate: Effectiveness of turning free-trial users into paying members.
Churn Rate: Identifying "at-risk" subscribers before they cancel.
Average Revenue Per User : Identifying opportunities for upselling and premium tiering. 
🛠️ Methodology & Strategic Pillars

Predictive Retention Modeling: Using behavioral data (e.g., declining login frequency) to trigger automated re-engagement campaigns.
Content Attribution: Analysing which specific titles or genres (e.g., Live Sports) drive the most initial sign-ups.
Cohort Analysis: Grouping subscribers by signup date or acquisition channel (e.g., Social Media vs. Direct) to identify long-term loyalty trends.
